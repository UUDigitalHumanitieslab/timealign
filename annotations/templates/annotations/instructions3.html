Instructions (part 3)

After you've selected a language pair, you will be presented with two fragments. The fragment on the right contains a construction in the source language, which is marked in green. On the left, you should find a translation of this fragment, and ideally, you are able to select a matching construction there.

Please select the matching construction on the right by clicking on 'ist' and 'gekommen'. The order of clicking is not important. After that, click the 'Submit' button to continue.
